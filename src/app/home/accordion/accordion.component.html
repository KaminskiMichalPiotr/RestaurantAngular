Chinese
Characterized by a diverse range of flavors and cooking techniques, including stir-frying, steaming, and braising. Popular dishes include dumplings, noodles, and rice.
French
Renowned for its elegance and refinement, French cuisine emphasizes rich, complex flavors and techniques such as braising, roasting, and sautéing. Popular dishes include coq au vin, boeuf bourguignon, and crème brûlée.
Italian
Famous for its use of fresh ingredients, such as tomatoes, olive oil, and cheese, Italian cuisine includes dishes such as pasta, pizza, and risotto.
Japanese
Known for its emphasis on fresh, seasonal ingredients and simple preparation methods, Japanese cuisine features dishes such as sushi, sashimi, and tempura.
American
Heavily influenced by European, African, and Native American cultures, American cuisine includes a variety of regional dishes, such as burgers, fried chicken, and barbecue.
